1 Executive Summary

APP fraud losses across UAE licensed financial institutions (LFIs) increased by 38.4% year-on-year in 2024, rising from AED 412M to AED 570M. Mobile banking emerged as the dominant channel, accounting for 63% of APP fraud losses. sq1

Fraud was concentrated among six LFIs, which together accounted for 57% of sector-wide APP losses despite representing only 21% of total digital transaction volume. sq4

2 APP Fraud Volume & Year-on-Year Trends

3 Channel Analysis

Mobile banking is the primary APP fraud vector in 2024, accounting for 63% (AED 360M) of total sector losses. Internet banking accounted for 27%, with telephone banking and branch at 10% combined. sq2

Mobile concentration is particularly elevated at EIB (79%), ABB (74%), and RAK (71%). sq2 sq4

6 Recovery Rates

The sector-wide APP fraud recovery rate in 2024 was 11.4% — below the GCC peer benchmark of 15–18%. Recovery rates were significantly higher at institutions with dedicated fraud operations teams (avg. 19.2%) vs. shared AML-fraud units (avg. 7.8%). sq5
