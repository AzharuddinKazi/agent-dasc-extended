Executive Summary
Across 47 UAE LFIs in Q1 2025, the peer median card fraud loss rate was 0.056% of total transaction volume. Six LFIs exceeded 2× the peer median, with Al Baraka Bank (ABB) recording the highest rate at 0.183% — 3.3× the median. Three of the six outliers are mid-tier retail banks with high digital-channel exposure; social engineering and card-not-present fraud account for the majority of losses in the outlier group.
Supervisory note: ABB, EIB, and FNB have remained above 2× peer median for two consecutive quarters. These institutions may warrant a targeted fraud control assessment under the CBUAE Fraud Prevention Supervision Framework.
